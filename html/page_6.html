How to Pay for Ads?

  1. Log into the Telegram ad platform using your Telegram account.
    https://ads.telegram.org
  2. Choose Budget » Add Funds on Fragment on the Telegram ad platform.
  3. Proceed with payment on Fragment using your TON. Learn more

How to Get Rewards from Ads

  1. On Telegram, open the profile page of your channel and go to Channel Settings .
  2. Choose Statistics » Monetization .
  3. If you have any available balance, select Collect .

How to Pay for Ads?

  1. Download and install a TON wallet, such as Tonkeeper or MyTonWallet . Check the full list of available wallets here
  2. Purchase some Toncoins (TON) on a crypto exchange such as OKX or ByBit — or using your bank card. Check the full list of exchange services here
  3. Transfer your Toncoins (TON) from the exchange service 2 to your wallet 1 .
  4. Choose Connect TON on Fragment to start using your funds.
